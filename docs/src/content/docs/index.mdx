## The Core Problem with Standard AI Chatbots

Imagine handing a 500-page legal contract to a detective and asking, *"How is the CEO connected to the breach of Clause 4?"* 

A human detective would read the contract, draw a mind-map linking the CEO to a subsidiary, linking the subsidiary to a vendor, and linking the vendor to Clause 4. 

Standard AI systems (known as **Retrieval-Augmented Generation** or **RAG**) do **not** do this. Standard RAG uses a "keyword matching" approach called Vector Search. It flips through the 500 pages, finds a paragraph that mentions "CEO", finds another paragraph that mentions "Clause 4", hands you both paragraphs, and hopes the AI can guess the connection. 

When the connections are complex, standard RAG fails. It misses the big picture, loses context, and worst of all, it hallucinates facts to fill in the blanks.

---

## The Solution: Cognitive Knowledge Engines

**DocRagGraph** completely reinvents how AI reads your documents by combining two incredibly powerful technologies:

1. **The Vector Database:** The traditional filing cabinet. It instantly finds exact quotes, definitions, and paragraphs.
2. **The Knowledge Graph:** The detective's mind-map. As the AI reads your document, it actively draws connections between people, places, concepts, and rules. 

### How it Works (Without the Jargon)

When you ask DocRagGraph a question, you aren't just querying a database. You are giving a task to an **Autonomous AI Agent**.

1. **Thinking:** The Agent looks at your question and decides its strategy. 
2. **Investigation:** If you ask a direct question (e.g., *"What is the fine for late payment?"*), it looks in the Vector Database. If you ask a complex question (e.g., *"Who is responsible if the supplier delays shipment?"*), it traces the literal connections in the Knowledge Graph.
3. **Self-Reflection:** Before answering, the Agent double-checks its findings. If the answer feels incomplete, it silently reformulates its search and tries again—looping until it has a mathematically verified, perfectly reasoned answer.
